ACADEMIC ACTIVITIES

Presentation of Final Projects of the Subject of Technical Drawing for Engineers, year 2015

My first-year students of the Electronic Engineering and Chemical Engineering majors presented on Friday 12-18-2015 their Computer Aided Design Drawing (CADD) skills using the software called AutoCAD as a tool. Here you can see a small sample of the final projects presented. During the semester they satisfactorily fulfilled the basic requirements and principles of instrumental drawing, they also fulfilled demanding 2D / 3D CADD tasks. These jobs are the product of days and nights of hard work and dedication. I am very proud to have had them as students in my class and very happy with their results. I wish you every success in your career.
